Our Goal

To help society's most vulnerable...

Save Children

There are more than 100 million orphans in the world. Most of these children do not have access to proper food, clothing or shelter.

Sponsor Now

As little as $20 a month can feed, clothe, shelter, educate and medicate an orphan in some countries. To them, your sponsorship could mean the world.

Global Reach

The funds we raise are use to support orphanages in 30 countries across 5 continents, through partnerships with various registered NGO's.

For and By the Children

Orphan Sponsorship Club is a youth-led interscholastic organization aiming to help orphans in third-world countries across the world. By reaching out to highschools accross the map, we hope to extend our impact and making a greater difference. Today, we are operational in 5 schools. Tomorrow, we'll be operational in hundreds.

Apply Now

Help Out

Partnered with University of Toronto's Orphan Sponsorship Program, we send the funds raised to where it is needed most. The charities that we are involved with includes Human Concern International, Islamic Relief and Islamic Circle of North America (ICNA) Canada, all of which are registered Canadian charities.
